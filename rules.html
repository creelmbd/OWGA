Okeana Whackers Golf Association

Official Rules & Format

League Overview

  • Location: Indian Ridge Golf Course
  • Day/Time: Friday evenings — the first group tees off at 5:00 PM

Entry & Fees

  • League Fee: $100 per player, due before the season starts
  • Weekly Green Fees: $32 per player
  • Optional Weekly Skins Game: $20 per team ($10 each, or one player can pay $20 and collect all winnings)

Format & Pairings

  • Play Style: 2-man scramble — both players hit each shot, choose the best ball, and then both play from that spot.
  • Odd Number Handling: If there is an odd number of players, the solo player plays two balls for each shot.
  • Weekly Pairings: Pairings are built from each player’s adjusted average and current season rank, with the best player paired with the lowest-ranked player and so on.
  • Season Rank: Based primarily on last year’s results and used for pairings and tie-breakers.
  • RSVP Deadline: Wednesday evening each week. Pairings and tee times are not guaranteed without a final RSVP. Any player marked TTD or with no response keeps the weekly pairings locked until updated.

Regular Season Scoring

  • Total Weeks: 14 weeks played
  • Scoring Weeks Counted: The best 6 scores count; higher scores are dropped.
  • Rainouts: After 2 rainouts, one score is dropped from the 6-score total (best 5 counted); after 3 rainouts, three scores are dropped (best 4 counted).
  • Rainout Adjustment: If a player has 5 counted scores and the 6th round is the final Friday rainout before the Championship, the highest score from that player’s completed rounds is added to their average.

League Rules & Conduct

Pace of Play

  • Keep up with the group ahead and aim to finish 9 holes in under 2 hours.
  • Maximum search time for a lost ball: 3 minutes.
  • Ready golf is encouraged — if it is safe and you are ready, hit.

Lie & Relief Rules

Lie Improvement
  • Place within 1 club length of the selected shot, in the same cut of grass (fairway-to-fairway, rough-to-rough, sand-to-sand).
  • No placing on the fringe or green if the original lie was rough, and vice versa.
Unplayable Lies
  • Base of a tree, exposed root, standing water (not casual), or embedded in thick vegetation: free relief within 1 club length, same condition.
  • Normal rough or a light tree line does not qualify.
Out of Bounds / Lost Ball
  • Lateral out of bounds: 1-stroke penalty; drop within 2 club lengths of the boundary last crossed, no closer to the hole.
  • Exception for Hole 7: if both balls are in the pond, re-tee with a 1-stroke penalty (stroke-and-distance).
Ground Under Repair
  • Free drop within 1 club length using course markings or group consensus.
Putting Surface
  • Mark and clean before putting. Both players may putt; if either makes it, the hole is out.

Miscellaneous

  • Tees: Men play from standard tees; seniors (55+) play from forward tees.
  • No mulligans.
  • All putts must be holed unless they are within putter grip and conceded.

Championship Rules

  • Format: 18 holes; partners switch after 9.
  • Participation: Participation is expected; missing the Championship may trigger a forfeit fee and affect payouts.
  • Payouts: Top 8 finishers, plus front-9 and back-9 champion partners.
